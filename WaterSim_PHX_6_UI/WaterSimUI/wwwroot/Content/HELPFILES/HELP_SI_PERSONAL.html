The Personal Water Use Sustainability Indicator

Personal Water Use Sustainability Indicator measures the level of personal water use in gallons per capita per day (GPCD). Decreasing the household water that you use on a personal (per capita) basis decreases the water demand for your community. Decreasing demand increases water sustainability and the water available for future use, by you or those that move into your community in the future. The minimum, attainable, (total) GPCD is 30.
