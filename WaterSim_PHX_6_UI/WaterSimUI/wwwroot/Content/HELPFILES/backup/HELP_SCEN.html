Sustainable Future Scenario to Simulate

Definition:The specific scenario to examine in a simulation.

Real World Example: The Sustainable Future Scenarios Project created seven scenarios of a possible water future. These include the "strategic", or business as usual scenario for currently planned policies and expected outcomes, the "Adaptive" which represent the response to extreme events, and the "Transformative" which may be classified as futures that are back-casted from radically transformed visions of sustainability.

Implementation: This drop-down control may be used to select one of the seven scenarios in this user interface. Simply click on the arrow next to the text box and a menu will open.

Field name: SCEN
