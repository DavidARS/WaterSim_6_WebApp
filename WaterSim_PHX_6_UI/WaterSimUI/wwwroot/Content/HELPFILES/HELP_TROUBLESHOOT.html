Trouble Shoot Your Session

    1. Nothing loads
      1. Nothing appears in my browser
        1. I have the correct URL (Go to item 10)
        2. I guess I typed in the wrong address (type in wsui9.quayapps.com)

    2. Images load
      1. I see images and charts but things appear scrambled
        • Go to item 10
      2. Some of the page can be viewed, but I need to scroll to see the rest
        1. I purposely set my zoom so that I can read small print
          • I will scroll
        2. I don't know anything about zooming!
          • Go to item 9 (below)

    3. I cannot find the HELP icons
      1. The model controls, indicators, and tabs have individual help files to provide more information or to clarify their use. These files are opened by clicking on the help icon

    4. I cannot find the settings Icon
      1. The settings icon is located in the "STATUS" bar at the bottom of the browser window (to the left of the "RUN MODEL" button)
      2. An additional settings icon can be found on the open settings panel. USE THIS icon to close the settings panel when you cannot find the original icon on the status bar

    7. You can change your personal zoom settings. The method to do so depends on the browser you are using.
      1. Chrome
        1. Navigate to the "settings" icon (the icon looks like three stacked lines in the tool bar located in the upper right hand corner of the window)
        2. Follow the menu to Zoom: increase or decrease the zoom by clicking on the + or - symbols, respectively until the view is what you are comfortable with
        3. These instructions work for version 36.0.1985.125 m and above
      2. Fire Fox
        1. Navigate to the "settings" icon (the icon looks like three stacked lines in the tool bar located in the upper right hand corner of the window-likely to the right of the address bar)
        2. Find Zoom in the menu items: increase or decrease the zoom by clicking on the + or - symbols, respectively until the view is what you are comfortable with
        3. These instructions work for version 18.0.1 and above
      3. Internet Explorer
        1. Navigate to the "settings" icon (this icon looks like a gear located in the tool bar located in the upper right hand corner of the window)
        2. Find Zoom among the menu options
        3. Hovering over the link enables you to zoom/zoom out, select a specific zoom level, or customize your zoom
        4. These instructions work for version 11 and above
